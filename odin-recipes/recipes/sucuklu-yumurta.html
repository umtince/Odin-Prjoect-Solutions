Sucuklu Yumurta

Description

Fried eggs with Turkish sucuk sausage is never going to be part of your healthy eating regime. But it makes a fabulous weekend breakfast or lunch treat. Sucuk & eggs is also a welcome addition to the village Turkish breakfast table.

Ingredients

  • 150 g sucuk outer skin removed & sliced into rings
  • 4 large free range eggs
  • 1 pinch of chilli flakes
  • salt & pepper to season
  • dessert spoon sunflower oil for frying

Steps

  1. Gently heat your sunflower oil in a frying pan.
  2. Add your sucuk and fry for a minute or so on each side until the juices release themselves.
  3. Now turn the heat low.
  4. Push your sucuk to the edges of your pan and crack the eggs into the pan.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and chilli flakes.

  7. Serve with fresh bread.
